Corporate History

Nikko was founded in 1908 in Kanazawa, the biggest city in the Hokuriku region of Japan, by Lord Maeda and local nobles.
The Maeda Local espacially fostered arts and crafts, and Kanazawa bacame a cultural center like Tokyo and Kyoto.
Alreay famous for our ironstone ceramis, our commitment to innvation has advanced greatly since 1970's.
Western materials such Bone China were introduced and developed using Nikko's own advanced technologies.
Today all aspects of production from glaze fomulation to mould making are manufactured in-house by our skilled team of craftspeopple.
The resulting pieces are some of the finest quality in the world.

Nikko Bone China

Nikko's white dinnerware brings out the utmost beauty of any type of food presentation.
With our exeptional technology, out Fine Bone China features a unique whiteness, which utilizes 50% bone ash to create a strong and luminous dinnerware.
In order to maintain its clarity, this pottery has a translucent finish.
Nikko Fine Bone China is highly valued by professionals for its beautifull whiteness and durability.

Products

Exquisite

Elegant engraved concentric lines frams food presentation on these refined contemporary bone china shapes.

Flash

A modern and elegant design with a thin and graceful shape. The shape and radiating lines bring contemporary feelilng without distracting from food presentations.

IO

IO is the design that blends cultural boundaries between East and West. Forms and shapes of IO represent a universal movement which travels freely between yin and yang.

UYUN

It has a characteristic smooth borderless form created in the image of the mystic spectacle of Salar de Uyuni or "the mirror of the heavens" in which all boundaries between sky and earth disappear.

Blossom

"Blossom" is synonymous with Japan, its fleeting beauty a metaphor for the transience of life. Bodo Sperlein creates a permanent record of it in Bone China.

Foam

Tableware designed by a chef for chefs. It was created to symbolize the clouds in the sky being soft, gentle, and harmonizing with sun.
